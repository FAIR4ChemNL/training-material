# IUPAC FAIR Chemistry Cookbook

* URL: <https://iupac.github.io/WFChemCookbook/intro.html>
* License: [Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike (CC-BY-SA) 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/)

The IUPAC FAIR Chemistry Cookbook is a self-serve web-based toolbox of interactive demonstrations of community best practices in machine-readable chemical data management. The Cookbook is an openly accessible collaborative resource to exemplify the FAIR data principles and standards in cheminformatics and provide hands-on executable content for experiential learning.
