# FAIR chemical structures in the Journal of Cheminformatics

* URL: <https://doi.org/10.1186/s13321-021-00520-4>
* License: [Creative Commons Attribution (CC-BY) 4.0](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/)

This journal article explains how any journal article can be made more FAIR by sharing a simple spreadsheet or CSV file with a few minimal fields describing the chemical structures. While proposed for that specific journal, is can be generally applied.
