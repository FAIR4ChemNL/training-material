# 2nd FAIR4ChemNL and NFDI4Cat Workshop

* When: 2026-10-13 – 2026-10-14
* Where: DECHEMA-Haus, Frankfurt am Main, Germany
* URL: https://www.tudelft.nl/en/faculty-of-applied-sciences/about-faculty/departments/chemical-engineering/principal-investigators/evgeny-pidko/evgeny-pidko-group/fair4chemnl/news-and-events/workshop

This workshop aims to reconvene scientists, data experts, and IT specialists to review progress, align ongoing initiatives, and define concrete next steps toward implementing FAIR data principles and unified data management solutions across the chemistry disciplines. The workshop is organized in a collaborative effort by the FAIR4ChemNL consortium (the Netherlands) and the NFDI4Cat consortium (Germany).
