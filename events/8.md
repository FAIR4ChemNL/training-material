# Yoda hands-on workshop

* When: 2026-04-22
* Where: UTR 2.01, SURF Utrecht, Moreelsepark 48, 3511 EP Utrecht, Utrecht, Netherlands
* URL: <https://www.surf.nl/en/agenda/yoda-hands-on-workshop>

This workshop will provide you with the knowledge you need to make your research data FAIR (findable, accessible, interoperable and reusable). You will experience the typical workflows for handling data and metadata in Yoda, an integral support tool for the whole data lifecycle.
